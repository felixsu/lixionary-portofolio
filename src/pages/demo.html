My Personal Passions

  • Photography: I've really dived into photography, spending hours honing my craft and developing my own unique style. You can see more of my work on my Instagram and my photography blog.
  • **Traveling:** Exploring new places and cultures whenever I get the chance is a huge passion. Some of my favorite adventures include trips to Bali, Lombok, Ijen Craters, Labuan Bajo, Papandayan Mountain, the Southern Java Coast, Ujung Kulon, Ujung Genteng, Sukabumi, Kelimutu Crater, Bromo Mountain, Tangkuban Parahu Crater, and Ambon.
  • **Baking:** When I'm looking for a creative outlet, you'll often find me in the kitchen. I particularly enjoy baking buns and love experimenting with different methods like Yudane and Thang Zhong to perfect my recipes.
  • **Tech Observer:** I love keeping up with the latest in technology, exploring both hardware and software. This includes diving into smartphone and OS evolution, as well as PC components like motherboards, memory, CPUs, and GPUs.
  • **Gaming:** Unwinding with a good game is always a great way to relax! I especially enjoy RPG (Role-Playing Games), simulation games, and turn-based strategy games.
